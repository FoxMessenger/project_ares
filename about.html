This website was created by four students at Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp.

The purpose of this app is to help you decide one movie to watch that is currently on Netflix. Everything is parsed through IMDB to ensure the rating of the movie is a 6 or above. Currently, in Netflix the user rating is not very accurate. We want to help users ensure an excellent viewing experience.
